Lamalambu akumele asetjentiswe  kubulala magciwane etandleni nobe kukuphi emitimbeni njengobe  lokushisa kungabanga kungaphatseki kahle kwesikhumba kufaka ekhatsi kuluma kwesikhumba. 
15.  Ngabe isebenta kanganani lemishini lesetjentiswa ekutfoleni bantfu lebahlaselwe ngulelivela kamuva leligciwane lekhorona (covid19)? 
Mishini lesetjentiswa kuhlola bantfu lebaneligciwane lekhorona iyasebenta ekuboneni bantfu labavelwe mukhuhlwane (sib,kushisa kwemitimba losezingeni losetulu kwengca lokwetayelekile) ngenca yekutseleleka ngeligciwane ikhorona. Ngakoke, angeke bakhone kubona bantfu labatselelekile kepha basengakakhombisi timpawu temukhuhlwane. Kungoba kutsatsa emalanga lasemkhatsini kwalamabili kuya kulalishumi ngaphambi kwekutsi labantfu labatselelekile bagule futsi babe nemukhuhlwane. 
16.  Ngabe lelivela kamuva leligciwane lekhorona  libatsikabeta bantfu labadzala, nobe nalabancane. 
Cha. bonke bantfu beminyaka lehlukene bangahlaselwa ngulelivela kamuva leligciwane lekhorona (covid19). 
17.  Ngabe akhona yini emakhambi lakasebentako ekuvikeleli nobe ekulapheni lelivela muva leligciwane lekhorona? 
Cha, emakhambi ukubulala magciwane (antibiotics) akasebenti kumagciwane lamancane, asebenta kumagciwane lamakhulu kuphela. 
18.  Ngabe likhona yini likhambi lelitsite lekuvikela nobe kwelapha lelivela kamuvakamuva leligciwane lekhorona(covid19)? 
Kute kube ngunyalo alikho likhambi lelitsite lelingavikela nobe kulapha lelivela muva leligciwane lekhorona (covid19).
